About

Chisholm Trail Firearms

Jeff Silverman has been collecting guns for over 30 years. His dream was to have a store that catered to collectors, pistol shooters, hunting enthusiasts and law enforcement alike. In 2011, that dream was turned into a reality. Here at Chisholm Trail Firearms, our goal is to turn back the clock to a time when good service meant something. The staff at Chisholm Trail Firearms know shooters needs and understands the gun enthusiast.
